Gabrielle Basulto — better known as GardenstateGab — is a dedicated and driven New Jersey Realtor specializing in areas from Essex County all the way down to Ocean County. With 9 years in the industry and over $37,697,701 in total sales volume, Gabrielle has built her business on results, relationships, and reputation. Known for her unmatched client service, 24/7 availability, and strong negotiation skills, she doesn’t rest until the job is done. Her clients don’t just close on homes — they become family.

Her mission is simple: to make every real estate experience — whether it’s your first, second, or third home — smooth, exciting, and seamless. She believes it is her duty not only to represent her clients to the best of her ability, but also to educate them along the way, empowering them to make confident and informed decisions.

Gabrielle genuinely cares about her clients. To her, every transaction tells a story, and every client becomes a lifelong friend.

Owning a summer home in Seaside Park, Gabrielle has managed her family’s three-family property for the past seven years. Meeting new guests weekly and showing them around town energizes her — hospitality and service come naturally.

Before real estate, Gabrielle worked as a Production Coordinator in NYC for major networks including MTV, Food Network, VH1, and CMT. Her favorite part of that career was building relationships and ensuring every project ran flawlessly from start to finish — a skill she seamlessly brings into every real estate transaction today.
